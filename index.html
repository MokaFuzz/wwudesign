Focus on your event.
Not your software.

Easy-to-use fundraising-event management solution for charitable organizations. SchoolAuction.net is built for Schools, Congregations, Clubs, and other Non-Profit Groups.

For Live, Silent, Mobile, and Online Auctions

Design the auction mix that’s right for your group – from small silent auctions to large galas to online auctions – we’ve got you covered. Mix online bidding, paper bid sheets, live auctions, and mobile bidding in any combination you want.

Easy, Fast Check-In and Check-Out

On auction night, your guests will spend their time bidding on items, supporting your organization, and having fun – NOT standing in long lines to check in or out. You can offer guests the option of checking in at home – and checking themselves out from their smart phone.

Save Money on Credit-Card Processing

Our software talks to just about any credit-card. If you have one you like, chances are good we can connect it to our software. Or shop around and find one with rates you like – and rest assured, we won’t mark those rates up or add any transaction fees on top of them.

Web-Based Software

SchoolAuction.net is web-based, so your team can work anywhere, from Macs, PCs, tablets, or smartphones.

Online Registrations

Sell tickets and sponsorships online, at your SchoolAuction.net event website.

Donation Database

SchoolAuction.net keeps track of critical info on your items and donors - info you can use year after year.

Flexible Check-In and Check-Out

Make things easy for your guests, let them check in from home, or at the event. At the end of the auction, they can check out and get their receipt from their smart phone or at your check-out desk.

Easily Generate Print Materials

Bid sheets, catalogs, gift certificates and more – quickly and easily generate all of the collateral you’ll need for your event.

Online and Mobile Bidding

Select items that you want to make available for online bidding before and after your event. You can also make selected items available for mobile bidding during your gala event.

Amazing Support

SchoolAuction.net offers a library of articles and videos to help you get the most out of our software. All subscribers have access to email-support prior to their event, and Premium subscribers receive two 1-hour private training sessions conducted via webinar.
