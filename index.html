About

Come Join Us!



WWU's design department invites you to join us for our 2018 Senior Portfolio show. Here 54 students will gather to show off their work ranging from graphics, motion, web, packaging, UX and other design fields. Come hang out and check out the work of our talented designers. We encourage you to bring your friends, family, next door neighbor, and even the person next to you to join us for this tremendous occasion. We would love to see you there.

Friday, June 15
Academic West 1-4PM

Guest Speaker Eike Koenig
AW 204 11:30-1PM

Jessica Karle



Jessica Karle is a designer and author currently based in San Francisco. She is the co-founder of prominent design publication, Kern and Bern and also serves the Head of Digital Design at Everlane. Alongside these accomplishments, Karle has worked many distinguished roles in companies such as Paperless Post and General Assembly. As a pioneer in digital design, come and hear Jessica talk about her experience in the industry.
